About Me

Hey! I'm Alexander "HyperNeutrino" Liao. I'm a 17-year-old Canadian teen in grade 12 with a passion for computer science, coding, music, math, debate, and video games.

I've been coding since grade 6, and I've used a wide range of languages. My preferred languages are Python, C++, and JavaScript, and I can also use Java though I don't like it. I also know some esoteric languages like Jelly.

I've written math contests since grade 2, and the Canadian Computing Challenge (CCC) since grade 8, with varying success. Some notable achievements include invitation to Repechage in 2018 and 2020, AIME in 2018, and CCO in 2019.

Music

Debate
